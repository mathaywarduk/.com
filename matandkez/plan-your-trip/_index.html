Getting there

If you’ve not yet decided how you’re getting up to Scotland, here’s some handy information to help you make a decision.

Drive

If you can, we’d recommend driving up and checking out the beautiful scenery around the Highlands.

It takes roughly…

  • 6.5 hours from Nottingham/Derby to Newtonmore
  • 5 hours from Lancashire to Newtonmore
  • 8.5 hours from London to Newtonmore
Get directions

Fly

The closest airports to Newtonmore are…

  • Inverness: 1 hour away
  • Edinburgh: 2 hours away
  • Glasgow: 2.5 hours away
Find flights

Train

The train will take you directly to Newtonmore or Kingussie and is approximately an 8hr journey from Nottingham/Derby and London.
Just enough time to have a few bottles of wine, play 20 rounds of rummy, and have a power nap.

Find train tickets

From London, Preston or Crewe you can also get the Caledonian Sleeper train, it takes a little bit longer but you travel through the night, arriving early morning with a full day still to go.
