The Roadtrip

We fancy a little explore of Scotland before we get married and anyone is welcome to join! We’re heading back to a place that we stayed in, Mhor 84, when looking for the wedding venue.

When

Wednesday 13th and Thursday 14th September.

Rooms & Price

There are 11 double/king/twin rooms and one cottage (2 double rooms). We’ve reserved the whole place for now, so let us know if you want a room and we’ll sort the booking. We’ll do this on a first come first serve basis, so shout up!
Do not fear, there is other accommodation nearby too.

The plan

Wednesday

Leave Nottingham in the morning and get there for the afternoon. Depending on when we arrive and the weather, we’ll probably… head out on a small walk, have dinner in the evening at the Motel’s restaurant and spend the evening chilling at the Motel bar.
Fingers crossed they’re still doing the burger and sticky toffee malteser pudding.

Thursday

Head out exploring for the day and then have dinner at the amazing chippy in the nearby village. Then back to the motel… Live music every Thursday “Thank Folk It’s Thursday”.
Wonder if we’ll get Bazza up there.

Friday

In the morning, we’ll head across to the wedding venue where we’ll be staying for a few days. No doubt we’ll have lots to do on the Friday afternoon, so we’ll see most of you again on the Saturday in the Chapel!

If you’re staying in Newtonmore or nearby for the wedding, it’s just under a 2 hour drive from the Motel.

The Motel

A great little find. Modern, stylish but super cozy restaurant with simple hotel rooms.
Food is served all day; a big range breakfasts, hearty lunches, cakes and a daily changing evening menu.

It has a games room with a pool table (Gareth), toys and board games. And roaring log fires for people to drink wine and fall asleep next to (Hayley).
