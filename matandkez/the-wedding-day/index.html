Getting to and from the wedding

It’s all taking place at the Glentruim Estate, set in 53 acres of woodland and gardens in the heart of the Scottish Highlands.

It was the first venue we visited and we started planning the wedding in our minds the moment we saw it. That was a good enough sign that it was the one.

Bus

For those staying in Newtonmore, we’ll arrange a bus pick you up and take you back. If you’re staying somewhere not on the recommended list, let us know and we’ll check the bus can swing by your way too.

Taxis

We’ve been advised they’re not a great option and don’t even exist in Newtonmore! You can book one from Aviemore, but it’s 30mins away so you’ll have a starting charge of about £20.
