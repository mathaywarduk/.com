Explore the area

Newtonmore is a small town but has a range of cycle and walking trails to explore. It’s also situated in the Cairngorms National Park which has plenty to do.

  • Local Pub: The Glen Hotel, Main St, Newtonmore, PH20 1DD
  • Nearest shops: Co-op, Balavil Brae, Newtonmore, PH20 1DR
  • Nearest supermarket: Tesco, Dores Rd, Inverness, IV2 4XT
    (1 hour away)

Aviemore

You’ll find Aviemore 30 mins away, a popular ski resort with more shops, bars and restaurants. If you head this way, you must visit our favourite pub/restaurant The Old Bridge Inn. The other one we’ve tried is The Winking Owl which also has great food, big dishes! Best to book ahead at both if you can.

Cairngorms National Park

Newtonmore is surrounded by The UK’s largest National Park which has everything from castles and distilleries to family attractions and endless outdoor fun, including watersports, snowsports, wildlife watching, and some incredible high and low level walking and cycling routes.

More info

Dalwhinnie Distillery

Dalwhinnie Distillery is just 3 miles from Newtonmore where some of the world’s most famous malts are made.

More info

The Wildcat Experience

Yes that’s right… painted stone cats! Different painted wild cats are dotted about Newtonmore. From the post office in the village, you can get a book of the ones to find and a sheet/map to tick them off.
Might be more fun after a few pints?

More info

Loch Insh Watersports Centre

Feeling adventurous? Loch Insh is about 20 minutes away and has sail sports, canoeing, kayaking, fishing, mountain biking, rowing, archery, play parks, and even a dry ski slope.
