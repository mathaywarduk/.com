The Roadtrip

If you’re joining us for the few days before the wedding, you might like to know the plan...

The plan

Wednesday 13th

Leaving Nottingham at 7am! Stopping en route for:

  • Breakfast at Cross Lanes Organic Farm - 9:30am
  • Lunch at Blair Dummond Smiddy - 2pm

Depending on when we arrive and the weather, we’ll probably… head out on a small walk, have dinner at the Motel’s restaurant and spend the evening chilling at the Motel bar.
Fingers crossed they’re still doing the burger and sticky toffee malteser pudding.

Thursday 14th

Before breakfast, Mat will be going for a swim in Loch Earn with anyone brave enough to join him! After breakfast, we’ll head out exploring for the day with a picnic lunch. Then back to the motel for dinner and live music (every Thursday is “Thank Folk It’s Thursday”).
Wonder if we’ll get Bazza up there.

Friday 15th

In the morning, we’ll be heading up to Newtonmore to check in for the wedding. We’ll see you in the Glen Hotel at 4pm for a quick drink, before an early night…

Newtonmore is just under a 2 hour drive from the Motel.

The Motel

A great little find. Modern, stylish but super cozy restaurant with simple hotel rooms.
Food is served all day; a big range breakfasts, hearty lunches, cakes and a daily changing evening menu.

It has a games room with a pool table (Gareth), toys and board games. And roaring log fires for people to drink wine and fall asleep next to (Hayley).
